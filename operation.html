Operation


Target The opponent you are locked on. Only one target at a time.
Durability The HP of the unit. If the counter reach 0, the unit will be destroyed.
Weapon recharge gauge The cooldown of the four weapons. You can use the weapon again if the gauge is full.
Ex gauge Special ability gauge. It will be accumulated when you cause or recive damage.
Method 1: Charge for the main weapon, and the three subweapons.
Method 2: Melee attack cancel.
Method 3: Evade or Guard escape from hitstun.
Boost gauge Used when you do a move other than standard walk, or during guard.
Energy gauge Used when you use energy consuming weapons.
Remaining lives Revival count for game modes using survival system.
Pilot image Display the primary image of the selected pilot.
Lock-on warning Yellow: an opponent lock-on on you ; Red: an opponent attack you
More lock-on warnings Yellow: an opponent lock-on on you ; Red: an opponent attack you
Pink: optical projectile ; White: ballistic projectile ; Blue : special attack
Kills The number of the destroyed opponents.
Score Gain points by attacking or defeating the opponents, but will be reduced
when you recive damage.
Radar Displays informations about the other units positions and lock-ons.


Operation methods
・Basics operations
Moving ↑,↓, ←,→
Targeting S (↑+S: Long distance lock ; ↓+S: Short distance lock)
●Hold S for 0.3 seconds to lock-on the oppenent that target you.
●Hold S for 0.6 seconds to disable the lock-on.
Jump Z
Guard V Hold down for guard. Guard cancel can be performed.

・Attack operations
Main weapon X (Can be charged up by holding down the button, then releasing it. Consumes Ex.)
Melee C (Press and hold ↑↓←→ while attacking to perform various combinations.)
Depending on the unit, there can be attacks other than melee.
Subweapon 1 A (Can be charged up by holding down the button, then releasing it. Consumes Ex.)
Subweapon 2 D (Can be charged up by holding down the button, then releasing it. Consumes Ex.)
Subweapon 3 F (Can be charged up by holding down the button, then releasing it. Consumes Ex.)

・Special operations
Dash Tap any movement key twice in the same direction. Hold to extend the lenght.
Boost Dash Press twice and hold Z.
Boost Dash long last the unit have boost. Can be change the direction with the ↑,↓, ←,→ keys.
Transformation Press V during Boost Dash.
　　Hold Z or V to continue. Can be change the direction with the ↑,↓, ←,→ keys.
　　Z: Rise ; V: Dive ; Z+V: Keep altitude
Attacking during Boost Dash All unit can use its primary weapon during Boost Dash.
Depending on the unit, it can use the subweapons or a variant of the subweapon.
Attacking during Transformation Depending on the unit, it can use its weapons during other forms.
The Transformation can completely alter the weapon's effect.
Guard Dash X during guard.
Counter from Guard C during guard. Can be done after you recive a hit during guard.
Recovery After you have been knocked down, X or C at the moment you hit the ground.
Landing guard cancel V at the moment you hit the ground. Consumes 1 Ex.
Emergency guard C+V during hitstun. You will be immediately changed to guard stance. Consumes 3 Ex.
Emergency evade X+V during hitstun. Guard Dash backwards. Consumes 2 Ex.
X+V while knocked down. Guard Dash backwards. Consumes 1 Ex.
Melee cancel After you hit your opponent, perform a Dash, Boost Dash, or guard to cancel your attack. Consumes 1/4 Ex.
Primary attack cancel Perform a Boost Dash while using the primary weapon.
OverClock Rapidly press V three times. Requires at least 4 Ex, consumes all available Ex over time.

・Other operations
HDR ON/OFF F4 (Only when the shader is enabled in the start-up screen.)
Shadow ON/OFF F5 (Only when the shader and the Shadow enabled in the start-up screen.)
Change warning display F6 (Only units → Disable all → Enable all in order.)
Chatlog display ON/OFF F7 (Chat must be on.)
Display multiplayer match informations Tab
Name of the player, used unit, equipped items, remaining lives, FPS, Ping.
Mark ready in the multiplayer lobby F1
Press again to unmark. If everyone marked as ready, the match begins.
Select Enter
Bring up menu during fight Esc
It is also pausing the game during singleplayer or local battle, but not stop the game during multiplayer.
Chat during multiplayer Enter (During this time, the unit is uncontrollable.)
Write the message, then press Enter again to send, and return to the unit control.
Team chat during multiplayer Ctrl+Enter
Team pilot communication Ctrl+0-9
Screenshot PrintScreen
Image is created in the ScreenShot folder. (PNG format will be used.)

・Basic combat
　Guard can be block only frontal attacks, use it wisely.
　Evade attacks with Dash and Boost Dash.
　Pay attention to the lock-on warnings. It help you to either guard or evade it.
　Use the Ex gauge to escape from hits and combos. Charge up your weapons to surprise your opponent. Activate OverClock to gain advantage.
